Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of alligator meat (cut into small chunks)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Flour for dredging
  • 1 cup of buttermilk
  • 1 cup hot sauce
  • 1 bottle of Ranch

    • Directions:

    1. Heat a deep fryer to 350 degrees
    2. Lightly season gator meat w/ salt and pepper prior to dredging in flour
    3. Combine buttermilk and hot sauce into 1 mixture
    4. Dip gator meat into buttermilk and hot sauce mixture and dip into flour again.
    5. Place gator meat in deep fryer until golden brown (more or less a few minutes)
    6. Drain on paper towels and serve with ranch

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 pounds of rattle snake
  • 1 tsbp Salt and pepper
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup of buttermilk
  • 1 cup cayenne pepper
  • 1 cup ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp of garlic and chile power
  • 1 tbsp of paprika
  • 1 cup cornmeal

    • Directions:

    1. Cut meat from the snake by cutting down the back
    2. Using tip of knife peel meat
    3. Once you have 2 long strips, tenderize with back of knife
    4. Cut strips into 1-inch pieces and place in bowl of buttermilk
    5. Mix to coat well
    6. In large bowl combine cornmeal and flour as well as the spices
    7. Dredge snake pieces in flour mixture and fry for 2 mins or until golden brown
    8. Repeat until all pieces are cooked. Transfer to paper towel lined plate
